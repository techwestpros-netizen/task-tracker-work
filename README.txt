Live Barcode Scanner

Files:
- index.html
- styles.css
- app.js

What changed:
- live barcode scanning through camera
- no photo capture step
- no OCR
- manual correction field
- local history in browser

How to use:
1. Replace your current index.html, styles.css, and app.js with these files.
2. Open the site on your phone.
3. Tap Open Camera.
4. Aim the barcode inside the scan box.
5. When it scans, save it to history.

Notes:
- Best over HTTPS
- Rear camera usually works best
- Small, blurry, or angled barcodes can still miss
